What's Lasciva Roma ?

Lasciva Roma is a web app to serve the results of Thibault Clerice's PhD Thesis about sexual vocabulary or isotopies in Latin literature from classical and late Antiquity. In this current beta version of the application, you'll find around 2500 excerpts of Latin Literature which were annotated based on the work of James Noel Adams, The Latin Sexual Vocabulary.
